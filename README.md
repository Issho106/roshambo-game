# roshambo-game
Preview Link: https://issho106.github.io/roshambo-game/

How to play:

1. An evil AI has taken control over your computer.
2. To regain back control to your computer, you need to beat the evil AI by winning 3 or more rounds out of 5.
3. A user can quit at any time (give up) by typing Q.
4. If the player wins back access to a device, he can restore his computer from evil AI and prevent it from taking control over other electronic devices.
